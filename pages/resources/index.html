What is QOM?
A monthly program led by SXC Physics Club in which we challenge physics enthusiasts with exciting physics questions, which not only enhances knowledge on physics but also helps develop problem solving skills, assisting as essential skills for different Olympiads. Each month there arrives a question from a specific topic from our physics course that requires creative solutions. The question is supposed to be solved and sent within the given time limit to the mail address of the club. Each participant will be awarded with a digital certificate and the correct answer will be rewarded with a certificate, cash prize and certain credits. The credits will later add up to make a physics enthusiast stand out as “SXC YOUNG PHYSICIST”. Below are the certain rules to be followed regarding the submission of the QOM !
